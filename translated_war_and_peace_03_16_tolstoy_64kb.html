﻿CHAPTER XVI

第十六章

Kutuzov accompanied by his adjutants rode at a walking pace behind the carabineers .

库图佐夫在副官的陪同下，以步行的速度骑马跟在卡宾枪队后面。

When he had gone less than half a mile in the rear of the column he stopped at a solitary , deserted house that had probably once been an inn , where two roads parted . Both of them led downhill and troops were marching along both .

当他在纵队后方走了不到半英里时，他在一座荒废的孤零零的房子前停了下来。两条路都是下坡路，部队正沿着两条路行进。

The fog had begun to clear and enemy troops were already dimly visible about a mile and a half off on the opposite heights . Down below , on the left , the firing became more distinct . Kutuzov had stopped and was speaking to an Austrian general . Prince Andrew , who was a little behind looking at them , turned to an adjutant to ask him for a field glass .

雾已经开始散去，对面高地约一英里半的地方已经隐约可见敌军。在左下方，枪声变得更加清晰。库图佐夫停了下来，正在与一名奥地利将军交谈。安德鲁王子在后面看着他们，转身向一名副官要了一个野战用的玻璃杯。

" Look , look !" said this adjutant , looking not at the troops in the distance , but down the hill before him . " It's the French !"

"快看，快看！"这位副官说，他没有看远处的部队，而是向眼前的山下望去。"是法国人！"

The two generals and the adjutant took hold of the field glass , trying to snatch it from one another . The expression on all their faces suddenly changed to one of horror . The French were supposed to be a mile and a half away , but had suddenly and unexpectedly appeared just in front of us .

两位将军和副官握住了野战玻璃杯，试图从对方手中抢过来。他们所有人脸上的表情突然变成了惊恐。法国人本应在一英里半以外，却出人意料地突然出现在我们面前。

" It's the enemy ?... No !... Yes , see it is !... for certain .... But how is that ?" said different voices .

"是敌人？不是！...是的，看到了！......对于某些....。但这是怎么回事？"不同的声音说道。

With the naked eye Prince Andrew saw below them to the right , not more than five hundred paces from where Kutuzov was standing , a dense French column coming up to meet the Apsherons .

安德鲁王子用肉眼看到，在他们的右下方，距离库图佐夫站立的地方不超过五百步的地方，有一支密集的法军纵队正在迎击阿普舍尔人。

" Here it is ! The decisive moment has arrived . My turn has come ," thought Prince Andrew , and striking his horse he rode up to Kutuzov .

"就在这里！决定性的时刻到了。轮到我了，"安德鲁王子想，他勒住马，骑到了库图佐夫面前。

" The Apsherons must be stopped , your excellency ," cried he . But at that very instant a cloud of smoke spread all round , firing was heard quite close at hand , and a voice of naive terror barely two steps from Prince Andrew shouted , " Brothers ! All's lost !" And at this as if at a command , everyone began to run .

"他喊道："阁下，必须阻止阿普舍龙人。但就在这一瞬间，一团烟雾向四周弥漫开来，近在咫尺的地方传来了枪声，离安德鲁王子只有两步之遥的地方，一个天真惊恐的声音喊道："兄弟们！一切都完了！"听到这一声令下，所有人都开始奔跑起来。

Confused and ever - increasing crowds were running back to where five minutes before the troops had passed the Emperors . Not only would it have been difficult to stop that crowd , it was even impossible not to be carried back with it oneself . Bolkonski only tried not to lose touch with it , and looked around bewildered and unable to grasp what was happening in front of him . Nesvitski with an angry face , red and unlike himself , was shouting to Kutuzov that if he did not ride away at once he would certainly be taken prisoner . Kutuzov remained in the same place and without answering drew out a handkerchief . Blood was flowing from his cheek . Prince Andrew forced his way to him .

混乱和不断增加的人群跑回了五分钟前军队经过皇帝身边的地方。不仅很难阻止这群人，就连自己也不可能不被他们带回去。博尔孔斯基只是努力不让自己与之失之交臂，他茫然四顾，无法理解眼前发生的一切。内斯维茨基满脸通红，怒气冲冲地对库图佐夫喊道，如果他不马上骑马离开，就一定会被俘。库图佐夫仍然站在原地，没有回答，而是掏出了手帕。他的脸颊流出了鲜血。安德鲁王子强行走向他。

" You are wounded ?" he asked , hardly able to master the trembling of his lower jaw .

"你受伤了？"他问道，几乎无法控制下颚的颤抖。

" The wound is not here , it is there !" said Kutuzov , pressing the handkerchief to his wounded cheek and pointing to the fleeing soldiers . " Stop them !" he shouted , and at the same moment , probably realizing that it was impossible to stop them , spurred his horse and rode to the right .

"伤口不在这里，在那里！"库图佐夫把手帕按在受伤的脸颊上，指着逃跑的士兵说。"拦住他们！"他喊道，同时可能意识到不可能拦住他们，便策马向右侧骑去。

A fresh wave of the flying mob caught him and bore him back with it .

新一波飞来的暴徒抓住了他，把他也卷了回去。

The troops were running in such a dense mass that once surrounded by them it was difficult to get out again . One was shouting , " Get on ! Why are you hindering us ?" Another in the same place turned round and fired in the air ; a third was striking the horse Kutuzov himself rode . Having by a great effort got away to the left from that flood of men , Kutuzov , with his suite diminished by more than half , rode toward a sound of artillery fire near by . Having forced his way out of the crowd of fugitives , Prince Andrew , trying to keep near Kutuzov , saw on the slope of the hill amid the smoke a Russian battery that was still firing and Frenchmen running toward it . Higher up stood some Russian infantry , neither moving forward to protect the battery nor backward with the fleeing crowd . A mounted general separated himself from the infantry and approached Kutuzov . Of Kutuzov's suite only four remained . They were all pale and exchanged looks in silence .

部队跑得密密麻麻，一旦被他们包围，就很难再脱身。一个人在喊道："快走！你为什么阻碍我们？""同一地点的另一个人转过身来向空中开枪；第三个人正在击打库图佐夫自己骑的马。库图佐夫费了九牛二虎之力才从这股人流的左侧逃了出来，他的队伍减员过半，他骑着马向不远处的炮火声冲去。安德鲁王子在逃亡的人群中挤出一条路后，试图靠近库图佐夫，却在浓烟中看到山坡上的俄军炮台仍在开火，法国人正向炮台跑去。在更高的地方站着一些俄国步兵，他们既没有向前保护炮台，也没有随着逃亡的人群向后退。一名骑兵将军脱离步兵，向库图佐夫走来。库图佐夫的随从只剩下四个人。他们都脸色苍白，默默地交换着眼神。

" Stop those wretches !" gasped Kutuzov to the regimental commander , pointing to the flying soldiers ; but at that instant , as if to punish him for those words, bullets flew hissing across the regiment and across Kutuzov's suite like a flock of little birds .

"拦住这些可恶的家伙！"库图佐夫指着飞来的士兵，喘着粗气对团长说，但就在这时，子弹像惩罚他的话一样，像一群小鸟一样，嘶嘶地飞过团部，飞过库图佐夫的套间。

The French had attacked the battery and , seeing Kutuzov , were firing at him . After this volley the regimental commander clutched at his leg ; several soldiers fell , and a second lieutenant who was holding the flag let it fall from his hands . It swayed and fell , but caught on the muskets of the nearest soldiers . The soldiers started firing without orders .

法国人袭击了炮兵连，看到库图佐夫后，正在向他开火。这一轮齐射过后，团长紧紧抱住了自己的腿；几名士兵倒下了，一名拿着军旗的少尉让军旗从手中掉了下来。旗帜摇摇晃晃地飘落，被最近的士兵的火枪击中。士兵们在没有命令的情况下开始射击。

"Oh! Oh! Oh !" groaned Kutuzov despairingly and looked around .... "Bolkonski!" he whispered , his voice trembling from a consciousness of the feebleness of age , " Bolkonski !" he whispered , pointing to the disordered battalion and at the enemy , " what's that ?"

"噢！噢！噢！"库图佐夫绝望地呻吟着，环顾....。"博尔孔斯基！"他低声说道，由于意识到年老体弱，他的声音有些颤抖。"博尔孔斯基！"他低声说道，指着混乱的营队和敌人，"那是什么？"

But before he had finished speaking , Prince Andrew , feeling tears of shame and anger choking him , had already leapt from his horse and run to the standard .

但还没等他说完，安德鲁王子就已经感到羞愧和愤怒的泪水在哽咽，他已经从马上一跃而起，跑向了标枪。

" Forward , lads !" he shouted in a voice piercing as a child's .

"前进，小伙子们！"他用孩子般刺耳的声音喊道。

" Here it is !" thought he , seizing the staff of the standard and hearing with pleasure the whistle of bullets evidently aimed at him . Several soldiers fell .

"在这里！"他想，抓住了标枪的杖，高兴地听到了子弹的呼啸声，显然子弹是瞄准他的。几个士兵倒下了。

"Hurrah!" shouted Prince Andrew , and , scarcely able to hold up the heavy standard , he ran forward with full confidence that the whole battalion would follow him .

"万岁！"安德鲁王子喊道，他勉强举起沉重的标枪，向前跑去，满以为全营的士兵都会跟着他。

And really he only ran a few steps alone . One soldier moved and then another and soon the whole battalion ran forward shouting " Hurrah !" and overtook him . A sergeant of the battalion ran up and took the flag that was swaying from its weight in Prince Andrew's hands , but he was immediately killed . Prince Andrew again seized the standard and , dragging it by the staff , ran on with the battalion . In front he saw our artillerymen , some of whom were fighting , while others , having abandoned their guns , were running toward him . He also saw French infantry soldiers who were seizing the artillery horses and turning the guns round . Prince Andrew and the battalion were already within twenty paces of the cannon . He heard the whistle of bullets above him unceasingly and to right and left of him soldiers continually groaned and dropped . But he did not look at them : he looked only at what was going on in front of him — at the battery . He now saw clearly the figure of a red - haired gunner with his shako knocked awry , pulling one end of a mop while a French soldier tugged at the other . He could distinctly see the distraught yet angry expression on the faces of these two men , who evidently did not realize what they were doing .

实际上，他只是独自跑了几步。一个士兵向前跑去，接着是另一个士兵，很快，整个营的士兵都喊着 "万岁！"向前跑去，超过了他。营里的一名中士跑上前去，接过安德鲁王子手中因重量而摇晃的旗帜，但他立即被打死了。安德鲁王子再次夺过旗帜，拽着旗杆，跟着营队跑了起来。在前方，他看到了我们的炮兵，其中一些人正在战斗，而另一些人则弃炮向他跑来。他还看到法国步兵正在抢夺炮兵的马匹，把火炮调转方向。安德鲁王子和他的营已经离大炮不到二十步了。他听到头顶上子弹的呼啸声不绝于耳，左右两边的士兵不断呻吟着倒下。但他没有去看他们：他只看着眼前发生的一切--炮台。他现在清楚地看到了一个红头发炮手的身影，他的军帽被打歪了，正拉着拖把的一头，而一个法国士兵正拽着拖把的另一头。他可以清楚地看到这两个人脸上痛苦而又愤怒的表情，他们显然没有意识到自己在做什么。

"What are they about?" thought Prince Andrew as he gazed at them . " Why doesn't the red - haired gunner run away as he is unarmed ? Why doesn't the Frenchman stab him ? He will not get away before the Frenchman remembers his bayonet and stabs him ...."

"他们在干什么？"安德鲁王子边看边想。"红头发的炮手手无寸铁，为什么不逃跑？法国人为什么不刺他？在法国人想起刺刀并刺中他之前，他是跑不掉的...."。

And really another French soldier , trailing his musket , ran up to the struggling men , and the fate of the red - haired gunner , who had triumphantly secured the mop and still did not realize what awaited him , was about to be decided . But Prince Andrew did not see how it ended . It seemed to him as though one of the soldiers near him hit him on the head with the full swing of a bludgeon . It hurt a little , but the worst of it was that the pain distracted him and prevented his seeing what he had been looking at .

果然，另一名法国士兵拖着火枪，跑到了挣扎着的人面前，红发炮手的命运即将被决定，他胜利地拿到了拖把，却仍然没有意识到等待他的是什么。但安德鲁王子并没有看到结局。在他看来，好像是他身边的一名士兵用重锤猛击了他的头部。有点疼，但最糟糕的是，疼痛分散了他的注意力，让他看不清自己在看什么。

" What's this ? Am I falling ? My legs are giving way ," thought he , and fell on his back . He opened his eyes , hoping to see how the struggle of the Frenchmen with the gunners ended , whether the red - haired gunner had been killed or not and whether the cannon had been captured or saved . But he saw nothing . Above him there was now nothing but the sky — the lofty sky , not clear yet still immeasurably lofty , with gray clouds gliding slowly across it . " How quiet , peaceful , and solemn ; not at all as I ran ," thought Prince Andrew —" not as we ran , shouting and fighting , not at all as the gunner and the Frenchman with frightened and angry faces struggled for the mop : how differently do those clouds glide across that lofty infinite sky ! How was it I did not see that lofty sky before ? And how happy I am to have found it at last ! Yes ! All is vanity , all falsehood , except that infinite sky . There is nothing , nothing , but that . But even it does not exist , there is nothing but quiet and peace . Thank God!..."

"这是怎么回事？我掉下去了吗？我的腿快不行了，"他想，然后仰面倒下。他睁开眼睛，希望看到法国人与炮手的搏斗是如何结束的，红发炮手有没有被打死，大炮是被缴获了还是被救走了。但他什么也没看到。在他的头顶上，现在只有天空--巍峨的天空，并不晴朗，但仍然无比巍峨，灰色的云朵在天空中缓缓滑过。安德鲁王子想："多么宁静、祥和、庄严啊！一点也不像我奔跑时那样，""一点也不像我们奔跑时那样，喊叫着、搏斗着，一点也不像炮手和那个满脸惊恐和愤怒的法国人挣扎着抢夺拖把时那样：那些云在无边无际的高空中滑行，多么与众不同啊！我以前怎么没有看到那蔚蓝的天空呢？我多么高兴终于找到了它！我终于找到了除了那无垠的天空，一切都是虚无，一切都是虚假。除了它，什么都没有，什么都没有。但即使它不存在，也只有宁静与和平。感谢上帝！......"
